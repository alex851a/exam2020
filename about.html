The Firm

Manifold is an architectural studio based in Copenhagen, Denmark. It's owned and directed by Architect MAA Pelle Brydegaard Sørensen, educated at The Royal Danish Academy of Fine Arts, School of Architecture. Manifold was established in 2004. The goal of Manifold is to contribute to the establishment of beautiful and extraordinary situations, where people experience the manifold wonders of life. The products of Manifold are architecture, educational events and text. Manifold switches between own projects and workd for other architectual firms, and the network of Manifold is under constant development. Through 'The Office of Multiplicity', Manifold gets inspiration and new perspectives. Welcome to a manifold world!
